1. Introduction

UTOPIA ("the App") is developed by Inferno Gurala Aka John Moses Gurala, based in India. This policy explicitly explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following when you use the App:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • University name and academic details
  • Attendance records (stored locally in user device) and timetables
  • User-uploaded files in Cloudinary
  • User published notes (community notes are visible publicly to other users; personal class notes are private to your account)
  • Habit tracking records
  • Device location data (for campus mapping, geofencing, and to display your approximate location to other students via the campus map feature)
  • Chat messages and connection/follow requests (exchanged with users you have accepted as connections)

3. Data Shared with Other Users

Some information you provide may be visible to other users of the App:

  • Your approximate location may be shown to other students via the campus map feature, if enabled. You can disable location sharing at any time in app settings.
  • Content posted to community notes (including shared PDFs and markdown notes) is visible to other users and may be edited or removed by other users in the same community space.
  • If you accept a connection/follow request, that user can send you chat messages and view information you've made visible to connections.

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or unaffiliated third parties.

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is synced to secure cloud infrastructure provided by Firebase and Supabase. Large files from the semester screen are stored in Cloudinary. No data is shared with unauthorized or unofficial third-party servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage)
  • Supabase (database and backend services)
  • Cloudinary (file storage service)

6. Advertising

The App is currently free with no advertisements. We plan to introduce ads approximately one month after the initial Play Store release. This policy will be updated before ads go live.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.
